1. Seniors are able to search for a volunteer in their area and chat with them 
2. anyone who would like to help can easily register to be one of the volunteers
3. recent announcments and updates on CoronaVirus in Canada

# HCI-Assignment3
A COVID-19 App that is to help seniors get help from a volunteer in the area
# Video Link:
 https://youtu.be/p_hH7MC4ul4
 https://youtu.be/fboaPnp7tpM 

# Public URL of Figma Design:
https://www.figma.com/file/vXmN4osfYP0uK9aYAEbOHK/Nedaa_-Alaji_Assignment1-Copy

# Top Features 
1. Seniors are able to choose a volunteer in their area and chat with them
2.	Anyone who would like to help can easily register to be one of the volunteers
3.	Seniors are able to choose the categories they need help in
4.	Seniors are provided with task scheduler to keep track of upcoming appointments
5.	Volunteers are able to check their volunteer hours and get notified on their upcoming events 
6.	recent announcements and updates on Coronavirus in Canada
7.	updated tips and guides to protect yourself against the virus
8.	the ability to connect with us or leave a comment that would help our app improve
